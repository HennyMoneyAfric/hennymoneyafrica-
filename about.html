🌍 About HennyMoney Afric

Afrobeat Artist • Songwriter • Digital Creator

My Story

HennyMoney Afric is an independent Afrobeat artist passionate about using music to inspire, unite and celebrate African culture.

Through original songs, creative content and digital platforms, the mission is to share positive messages, African pride and global connection through music.

Mission

To create impactful music that promotes unity, hope, culture and opportunity while building a strong global community around African creativity.

Vision

To become a recognized Afrobeat brand that connects people through music, culture, entertainment and digital innovation.
