Data Deletion Instructions

If you want your data deleted from HennyMoney Afric systems or Meta-connected features, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to hennymoneyafric@gmail.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request.

Please Include

  • Your full name
  • The Facebook account or page information connected to the request
  • A short description of the data or integration you want deleted

What Happens Next

We will review the request and delete eligible data within a reasonable period, subject to legal, security, or record-keeping obligations.

Contact

Email: hennymoneyafric@gmail.com
